Mandala Flower:

I made this project by using the shoes in Photoshop and rotating them constantly to make patterns. First I chose the shape I wanted, which was a flower. After I duplicated the pages and made the image smaller and changed the color and centered it then rotated it and combined it. Lastly I repeated the steps until I made a spiral. I used a beautiful flower and the colors I used were dark blue and light green. What I notice happen is the shapes kept duplicating and shifting the shapes everywhere.  

Filters & Adjustments Transformation:

We made this project by using photoshop and turning a picture into a really creative artwork. First what I did was choose an image that I preferred. Then edit it by rotating it to make it look like a pattern. After that, combine it to make it look like a repeated pattern on it and it goes from a simple image to looks like a unique pattern as a rug/mat. The original photo was making copies and rotating them and adding them together to make this unique pattern.
